About WeightPlay

WeightPlay is a mobile-first web game platform created by WeightStudio.

Our goal is to build a growing collection of original browser games for kids, families, and casual players. Games are designed to be easy to start, friendly on phones, and suitable for short play sessions.

What We Build

  • Original web games that can be played from the browser.
  • Family-friendly games with clear age and topic categories.
  • Animal games, puzzle games, arcade games, and educational games.
  • Mobile-first experiences that do not require account registration for basic play.

Platform Direction

WeightPlay is not a single game. It is a long-term platform that will continue adding new games, improving existing games, and expanding localization, accessibility, and player-friendly features.
