Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

WeightPlay is operated by WeightStudio. This policy explains how WeightPlay handles information when visitors use our website and browser games.

Information We Store Locally

Some games may save progress, settings, scores, coins, equipment, or language preferences in the browser using local storage. This data stays on the visitor's device unless the browser or device syncs it separately.

Analytics

WeightPlay may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand page visits, game starts, completions, device type, country-level usage, and similar aggregate behavior. We use this information to improve games and platform quality.

Advertising

WeightPlay may use Google AdSense or related Google advertising products in the future. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show, measure, and improve ads according to their own policies.

Children and Families

WeightPlay includes family-friendly games. We do not require account registration for basic play and do not knowingly ask children to submit personal contact information through the games.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as GitHub Pages, Google Analytics, Google AdSense, or browser platform features. These services may process technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact WeightStudio through the official contact page.
